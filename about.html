The digital Spark

Inspired by online potential, I self-taught web development (WordPress, PHP, HTML, CSS) for my blog. landing that first Google AdSense approval, was the real start.

The birth of a Designer

Those standard website templates in wordpress just weren't cutting it for my blog. I started playing around with Photoshop, and a real passion for design sparked. I dove into design principles, and honestly, I loved creating visuals that actually looked good.

Early professional venture

My first gig was at a startup, where I was the design and front-end person. I got to build websites from the ground up, seeing my designs go from concept to live. It was a pretty cool experience, seeing my ideas come to life.

Navigating the Streaming Landscape at Muvi

Working on a major streaming platform, with clients like Sony, was a serious eye-opener. I learned a ton about UX in a fast-paced environment, designing for a massive audience. Plus, it was a fantastic chance to blend coding and design, and I also enhanced my design knowledge by participating in Google Developer Group events.

Designing for Data Visualization at FusionCharts

At FusionCharts, used by 28,000 companies and 800,000+ developers, I shifted gears to focus purely on design. I worked on optimizing user experiences and creating a new design system, which was published by The Startup. FusionCharts was acquired later and the re-design experience played a key role.

Shaping Expense Management at Fyle

Leading the design team at the expense management platform was a big step. I got to mentor other designers and play a key role in the product's success. It was rewarding to see the team grow and contribute to achieving PMF and PLG. I also worked closely with the founders, which helped me develop valuable business acumen, and especially to see the business grow from 0 to $8M+.

Building Portfolio

For this portfolio, I decided to go back to my coding roots. I rolled up my sleeves and built it from scratch using basic HTML, CSS, and Bootstrap. Honestly, I kind of hacked it all together to bring this to life, and I'm pretty proud of it.
